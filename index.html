Prof. Ahmad Pervez is working as a Professor in Zoology Department, and Director (Research Studies) at Pt. L.M.S. Rishikesh Campus, Sri Dev Suman Uttarakhand University. He received Bhakt Darshan Higher Education Honour Award for Science from Govt. of Uttarakhand, and the Excellent Teacher Award for Zoology on the occasion of the 3rd International Conference on Sustainable Development Initiatives in South East Asia in Kathmandu, Nepal. Furthermore, he is a recipient of the Young Scientist Award in Zoology from the 3rd Uttarakhand State Science and Technology Conference held at IIT, Roorkee. He has published one book, 101 research papers, 30 book chapters, and 5 popular science articles in reputed journals. He is an Associate Editor of a prestigious international journal, Frontiers (Frontiers in Ecology and Evolution), Executive Editor of Annals of Science and Allied Research, Section Editor, Probe (Animal Science) from Universe Scientific Publisher, and on editorial boards on many academic journals. He visited SIX countries (Italy, Canada, Malaysia, Thailand, Nepal and Indonesia) and is a Life Member of SIX academic bodies. He has completed FIVE (4 Major and 1 Minor) research projects funded by SERC, SERB and UGC and is associated with more than TWENTY-FIVE research journals of international repute, as a reviewer. He participated in more than FIFTY International and National Conferences and is a Coordinator of the Oriental Region for preparing a list of Endangered and Flagship for the Conservation of Ladybird species by the International Union for Conservation of Nature Species Survival Commission (IUCN SSC). Under his supervision, FOUR Ph.D.s have been awarded, TWO are in the submission phase, while TWO are ongoing.
